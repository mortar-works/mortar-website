## Stronger Teams

Equip your teams with the tools, data, and workflows they need to work more effectively — and deliver better outcomes together.

**Great services are built by great teams.** But too often, the people on the frontline of public and social services are held back by systems that weren't designed for them. They are working with outdated tools, navigating siloed information, and spending more time on administration than on the work that matters. The result is burnout, inefficiency, and outcomes that fall short of what both teams and the people they serve deserve.

Mortar helps organisations build the digital infrastructure that enables their teams to do their best work — efficiently, collaboratively, and with the information they need at their fingertips.

### The Challenge

Frontline workers in health, housing, social care, and community services face an increasingly complex operating environment. Caseloads are rising, resources are constrained, and the needs of the people they support are growing more complex. Yet many organisations are still relying on systems built for a simpler era — systems that fragment information, create unnecessary friction, and fail to support collaborative working.

The gap between what teams are capable of and what their tools allow them to do is one of the most significant barriers to better outcomes in public services.

### How We Help

We design and build tools that work the way your teams work — reducing friction, surfacing the right information, and enabling more effective collaboration.

- **Case management tools:** We build case management systems designed around the actual workflows of frontline practitioners — not generic templates that have to be forced to fit.
- **Information access:** We ensure practitioners have access to the full picture of the people they support — drawing together data from across your systems into a coherent, usable view.
- **Workflow automation:** We identify and automate the repetitive administrative tasks that consume team time, freeing people to focus on the work that requires human judgement.
- **Collaboration infrastructure:** We build the shared platforms and communication tools that enable teams to work together effectively — across locations, disciplines, and organisational boundaries.
- **Team analytics:** We provide teams and managers with insight into workload, activity, and outcomes — enabling better resource allocation and earlier identification of team-level issues.

### Key Outcomes

- **Efficiency:** Reduce the time practitioners spend on administration, giving them more time for direct support and meaningful work.
- **Insight:** Give practitioners and managers a clearer picture of caseloads, needs, and progress — enabling better decisions about how to deploy their skills and time.
- **Collaboration:** Break down the silos that prevent teams from working together effectively, enabling more joined-up responses to complex needs.
- **Wellbeing:** Reduce the frustration and burnout that comes from working with poor tools — supporting team retention and performance.

### Who This Is For

Stronger teams is relevant for any organisation with frontline workers operating in complex environments. Our work in this area spans children's services, housing support, community health, and adult social care — wherever better tools translate directly into better support for people.
