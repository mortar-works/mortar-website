## Smarter Decisions

Use data and intelligence to make faster, more confident decisions — grounded in evidence and designed to deliver better outcomes.

**Organisations face more decisions than ever before.** Resource allocation, service design, investment priorities, risk management — every one of these requires good information, clear analysis, and the confidence to act. Yet too many organisations are still making high-stakes decisions based on incomplete data, disconnected systems, and manual processes that slow everything down.

Mortar helps organisations build the data infrastructure and intelligence capability they need to make smarter decisions at every level — from operational choices on the frontline to strategic calls in the boardroom.

### The Challenge

Decision-making in public sector and social impact organisations is hard. Data is held across multiple systems that don't talk to each other. The analysis needed to draw meaningful conclusions is time-consuming and requires specialist skills. And the pressure to act quickly often means decisions are made before the full picture is clear.

The result is decisions that are slower than they need to be, less well-informed than they should be, and harder to justify to boards, funders, and the people you serve.

### How We Help

We work with organisations to connect their data, apply intelligence, and surface the insight that matters most to the decisions they face.

- **Data consolidation:** We bring together data from across your systems into a single, coherent view — eliminating the silos that slow decision-making down.
- **Intelligent analysis:** We apply machine learning and advanced analytics to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and surface the signals that matter most.
- **Decision support tools:** We design and build tools that put the right information in front of the right people at the right time — from dashboards for senior leaders to case-level insight for frontline practitioners.
- **Evidence frameworks:** We help organisations build the evidence frameworks they need to make decisions consistently and account for them clearly.

### Key Outcomes

- **Speed:** Reduce the time it takes to gather, analyse, and act on information — enabling faster decisions without sacrificing quality.
- **Confidence:** Make decisions grounded in evidence rather than assumption, with clear audit trails that support accountability.
- **Consistency:** Apply consistent decision criteria across teams, locations, and cases — reducing variability and improving fairness.
- **Impact:** Allocate resources where they will make the greatest difference, informed by data that shows what works and for whom.

### Who This Is For

Smarter decisions is relevant for any organisation that needs to act quickly and effectively on complex information. Our work in this area spans local government, housing, health, and the voluntary sector — wherever data-driven decision-making can improve outcomes for people and communities.
