> We are building intelligent systems to power the next generation of products and solutions. If that mission resonates with you, come pay us a visit.

Mortar is a highly ambitious multi-discilinary team committed to redefining relationships between people, property and places. We see a future in which our technology powers every system and service. We are always looking for people who share our commitment to technology and making significant impact. 

If any of the roles below feel right for you, get in touch.

---

## Associate — Children and Young People

We are looking for an experienced practitioner to join us as an Associate working alongside both our client and our product teams. You will bring deep knowledge of children's services and the ability to translate that expertise into clear product requirements and sharp insight during client engagements. You need to be comfortable in a fast-moving environment and interested in how intelligent software can better engage children, young people and those that support them.

---

## Associate — Dementia Friendly Venues

We are looking for an Associate with expertise in inclusive design to work with us on our dementia friendly accreditation platform and related digital tools. You will bring the practical knowledge to help us build better software; contributing to product design, client engagements, and the development of our frameworks and platforms. Experience in assessment, training, or occupational therapy is ideal.

---

## Associate — Digital Skills and Employability

We are looking for an Associate with expertise in digital skills and employment support to contribute to the design and development of our digital skills and employability tools. You understand the barriers to digital participation and economic opportunity, and you can translate that knowledge into product insight and evidence-based thinking. You are as comfortable in a product design session as you are working directly with commissioners and service providers.

---

## Product Manager — Hoop'd

We are looking for a Product Manager to own the roadmap for Hoop'd, our intelligent platform for connecting people and places. You will set priorities, work closely with users and clients to turn insight into well-specified requirements, and keep our design and engineering teams focused and moving. You have shipped software before, you understand the trade-offs of a growing product, and you know how to balance new features with design functionality and user feedback.

---

## Junior Designer

We are looking for a Junior Designer to join our design team. Someone with strong foundations in visual or interaction design, fluency in design programmes, and genuine curiosity about complex, real-world problems. You will work alongside senior designers across our full range of work: product interfaces, service design, and brand. You are detail-oriented, collaborative, open to feedback, and ready to learn by doing on meaningful projects.
