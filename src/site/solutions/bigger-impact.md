## Bigger Impact

Scale your work, demonstrate your value, and maximise the difference your organisation makes to the people and communities you serve.

**Impact is the reason organisations exist.** Whether you are a local authority delivering statutory services, a housing provider managing thousands of homes, or a social enterprise working with marginalised communities, the measure of your success is not outputs or activity — it is the difference you make in people's lives. Yet many organisations struggle to measure, communicate, and maximise their impact in ways that drive real change.

Mortar helps organisations build the data infrastructure, measurement frameworks, and intelligence capability they need to understand, demonstrate, and grow their impact at scale.

### The Challenge

Demonstrating impact is genuinely hard. The outcomes that matter most — improved wellbeing, sustained independence, stronger communities — are difficult to measure and often take years to materialise. In the meantime, organisations are under pressure to prove their value to commissioners, funders, and the public with data that is incomplete, inconsistent, or captured in ways that don't reflect what actually matters.

At the same time, scaling impact is its own challenge. Solutions that work brilliantly in one place often fail to transfer — because the digital infrastructure, the data capability, or the governance frameworks needed to scale them simply don't exist.

### How We Help

We work with organisations to build the foundations for bigger impact — connecting data, measuring outcomes, and designing for scale from the outset.

- **Outcomes measurement:** We design measurement frameworks that capture the outcomes that matter — not just the outputs that are easy to count — and build the data infrastructure to collect, analyse, and report on them.
- **Impact reporting:** We build reporting tools that translate complex outcome data into clear, compelling evidence for commissioners, funders, boards, and the public.
- **Scale architecture:** We design digital solutions with scale in mind from the start — building the technical architecture that allows a solution to grow from a local pilot to a national platform without losing quality or coherence.
- **Learning systems:** We build the feedback loops and learning infrastructure that allow organisations to understand what is working, adapt what isn't, and continuously improve their impact over time.

### Key Outcomes

- **Evidence:** Build a robust, credible evidence base for your work that stands up to scrutiny from commissioners, funders, and regulators.
- **Accountability:** Demonstrate clearly how resources are being used and what difference they are making — to the people you serve, to your funders, and to the public.
- **Scale:** Grow your most effective solutions without losing the quality and responsiveness that made them work in the first place.
- **Learning:** Create the conditions for continuous improvement — so your organisation gets better at delivering impact over time, not just bigger.

### Who This Is For

Bigger impact is relevant for any organisation that wants to do more with what it has — and demonstrate clearly that it is doing so. Our work in this area spans local government, housing associations, integrated care systems, and the voluntary and community sector.
