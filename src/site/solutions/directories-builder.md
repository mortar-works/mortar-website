## Directories Builder

Build the local area infrastructure that connects people to the services, resources, and opportunities they need.

**Our Directories Builder** enables organisations to create, populate, and maintain dynamic, searchable directories of local services, provision, and resources. From alternative education provision and digital inclusion resources to cultural events and community health services, the platform gives administrators and service providers the tools to keep information accurate, accessible, and actionable — without placing an unsustainable management burden on a central team.

### How it Works

Administrators define the structure and categories of the directory. Service providers and partners are given their own access to create and maintain their listings directly, reducing the central overhead of keeping information up to date. Users — whether members of the public, practitioners, or partner organisations — can then search, filter, and access the directory in a format designed for their context.

- **Structured listings:** Define consistent templates for service, resource, and opportunity listings — ensuring every entry contains the information users need.
- **Provider self-management:** Give service providers the tools to maintain their own listings, keeping the directory accurate without requiring central administration for every update.
- **Searchable and filterable:** Users can search and filter by type, location, need, age group, and other criteria relevant to your service context.
- **Integration-ready:** Connect your directory to triage and assessment tools, enabling automated recommendation of relevant resources based on individual need.

## Key Features

### Customisable Directory Structure
Define the categories, fields, and filters that reflect your service landscape. Whether you are building a directory of alternative education provision, community health services, digital inclusion resources, or local cultural events, the structure adapts to your context rather than forcing your context to adapt to it.

### Provider Self-Service Administration
Empower your network of service providers to manage their own presence in the directory. Providers can create, update, and manage their listings through a simple, accessible interface — reducing the administrative burden on your central team and improving the accuracy and freshness of information across the directory.

### Geospatial Mapping
Surface directory listings on an interactive map, enabling users to find services and resources that are accessible from their location. Particularly valuable for large geographic areas where proximity to provision is a key factor in access.

### Verification and Quality Control
Administrators retain oversight of what appears in the directory. Listings can be set to require approval before publishing, ensuring quality and accuracy are maintained even as the directory grows and providers manage their own content.

### Global API Integration
Extend the reach of your local directory with national resource feeds. Our global API system provides access to accredited national directories — including digital inclusion resources and housing advice — supplementing local provision and filling gaps where local services are not yet developed.

### Analytics and Reporting
Understand how your directory is being used. Track which services are viewed most frequently, identify gaps where demand is not matched by available provision, and build the evidence base for service development and commissioning decisions.

## Benefits

- **Accurate, up-to-date information at scale**
   Distributed self-management by service providers removes the bottleneck of central administration — keeping information current without creating an unsustainable workload for your team.

- **Better access for users and practitioners**
   A well-structured, searchable directory dramatically reduces the time it takes for practitioners to identify appropriate services — and gives users the confidence to navigate provision independently.

- **The foundation for intelligent signposting**
   When connected to triage and assessment tools, your directory becomes the engine of personalised recommendation — matching individual needs to the most relevant resources automatically.

- **Evidence of need and provision**
   Data on directory usage reveals where demand is concentrated and where provision is missing — supporting advocacy, commissioning, and investment decisions.
