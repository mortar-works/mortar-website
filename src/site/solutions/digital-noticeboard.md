## Digital Noticeboard

Reach your community with accurate, accessible, up-to-date information — wherever they are.

**Our Digital Noticeboard** is a managed platform for publishing and distributing community information, local events, and service updates in accessible formats across digital and print channels. Designed for organisations that need to communicate consistently with a diverse community — including those with lower digital confidence, limited mobility, or communication preferences that span print, screen, and in-person — the platform creates a single, managed source of truth that feeds every channel.

### How it Works

Administrators and partner organisations publish information through a simple, shared content management environment. The platform then formats and distributes that content across configured channels — digital screens, web listings, printable cards, email communications, and partner networks — ensuring that the same accurate information reaches users in the format most accessible to them.

- **Single source of truth:** Manage all community information from one place, eliminating the inconsistency of maintaining multiple separate channels.
- **Multi-channel distribution:** Publish once and distribute across digital screens, websites, print, and partner networks simultaneously.
- **Accessible formats:** Information is structured and formatted to meet accessibility standards, with uniform cards that are recognisable in both print and digital form.
- **Partner contributions:** Service providers and community organisations can contribute their own listings directly, distributing the management effort and keeping information current.

## Key Features

### Accessible Information Design
Every listing is formatted using our uniform service card design — a consistent, recognisable format that works across digital and print contexts. Cards are structured to include the information users need to act on an opportunity or access a service, in a format that builds familiarity and confidence over time.

### Multi-Channel Publishing
Publish information once and have it appear wherever your community looks for it. Digital noticeboards in community centres, libraries, and health settings; web listings accessible from any device; printable cards for face-to-face engagement; and feeds into partner communications systems — all managed from a single administration environment.

### Partner and Provider Network
Enable community organisations, cultural partners, and service providers to contribute their own events, resources, and opportunities to the noticeboard. A simple, accessible submission interface means partners can maintain their own presence without requiring central administration, while approval workflows ensure quality is maintained.

### Event and Campaign Management
Manage time-limited events and recurring activities alongside permanent service listings. The platform supports event-based content with specific dates, times, and locations, as well as campaign pages for focused communication initiatives — all within the same managed environment.

### Geospatial Mapping
Surface noticeboard listings on an interactive map, helping community members find opportunities and services that are local and accessible to them. Particularly valuable for communities spread across a large geographic area.

### Engagement Analytics
Understand how your community is engaging with the noticeboard — which listings attract the most interest, which channels are most used, and where there are gaps between the information being published and the needs of different community groups. Use this data to improve your communications and make the case for investment.

## Benefits

- **Consistent, trusted information**
   A single source of truth means the same accurate information reaches users across every channel — eliminating the confusion and frustration of out-of-date or inconsistent messaging.

- **Reduced management overhead**
   Distributed content management by partner organisations dramatically reduces the burden on a central team while maintaining quality through approval workflows. Publish information once; reach every channel.

- **Greater reach for isolated communities**
   Multi-format distribution — including print and in-person materials — ensures the noticeboard reaches community members who are not digitally confident or connected, without sacrificing the efficiency of a digital system.

- **Stronger partner relationships**
   Giving community organisations the tools to promote their own activities builds engagement and investment in the platform — creating a richer, more comprehensive information environment for everyone.
