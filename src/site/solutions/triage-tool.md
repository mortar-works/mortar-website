## Triage Tool

Assess user needs quickly and accurately — and deliver the right support at the right moment.

**Our Triage Tool** enables organisations to conduct structured needs assessments at the point of first contact, using custom algorithms to generate personalised recommendations and referral pathways. Whether delivered digitally by the user or supported by a practitioner in person or by phone, the tool creates a consistent, data-driven starting point for every engagement — reducing inconsistency, improving outcomes, and generating the evidence needed to understand your population's needs.

### How it Works

The Triage Tool guides users or practitioners through a structured assessment, capturing the information needed to understand a person's situation and priorities. Our algorithm applies that information against your defined criteria to generate a tailored set of recommended resources, services, or next steps — drawing on both national directories and your local service offer.

- **Structured assessment:** Custom question sets designed around your service context and user needs.
- **Algorithm-driven recommendations:** Automated matching of user needs to relevant resources, services, and pathways.
- **Assisted and self-service modes:** Can be completed independently by users or conducted through assisted capture with a practitioner.
- **Local and national resources:** Integrated access to local directories and national resource APIs for comprehensive signposting.

## Key Features

### Custom Assessment Design
Build assessment journeys tailored to your service context — from digital inclusion and housing risk to school exclusion and health needs. Question logic adapts dynamically based on responses, ensuring every user reaches a relevant, personalised outcome.

### Algorithm-Driven Matching
Our matching engine applies your defined criteria to assessment responses, ranking and surfacing the most relevant resources and referral options. As your local directory grows, the quality of recommendations improves with it.

### Practitioner and Self-Service Modes
The tool is designed for flexibility. Users can complete assessments independently online, or a practitioner can work through the assessment with them — capturing the same structured data and generating the same quality of recommendation regardless of how the session is conducted.

### Progress Tracking and Follow-Up
Monitor where users are in their journey, track which resources they have been referred to, and identify those who need further support. The tool creates a longitudinal record of engagement that supports case management and continuity of care.

### Data Visualisation and Reporting
Access real-time data on assessment activity, needs profiles, and referral outcomes across your population. Understand demand patterns, identify gaps in provision, and build the evidence base for commissioning decisions.

## Benefits

- **Consistency at scale**
   Every user receives a structured, evidence-based assessment — regardless of who is conducting it or where. Reduces variability in service delivery and improves equity of access.

- **Earlier intervention**
   By identifying needs accurately at first contact, the tool enables earlier and more targeted support — reducing the likelihood of escalation and crisis.

- **Reduced practitioner workload**
   Automated assessment and recommendation frees practitioners from manual signposting, allowing them to focus time and attention on those with the most complex needs.

- **Rich population data**
   Every assessment generates structured data. Over time, this builds a comprehensive picture of need across your population — informing service design, commissioning, and advocacy.
